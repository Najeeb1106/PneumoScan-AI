How It Works

A simple 3-step process from upload to diagnosis.

1

Upload Image

Navigate to the secure dashboard and upload a standard JPEG or PNG format chest radiograph. Ensure the image is clear and well-lit.

2

Tensor Processing

The uploaded image is automatically resized to exactly 150x150 pixels, normalized, and transformed into a tensor. This is then fed into our trained Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) architecture.

3

View Results

Within milliseconds, receive a definitive classification (Pneumonia vs. Normal) alongside an interactive probability graph demonstrating the model's confidence.
